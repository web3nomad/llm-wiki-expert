atypica.ai is an AI research agent that simulates consumer behavior. Instead of running traditional surveys or focus groups, atypica creates AI-powered synthetic consumers — called Real Person Agents — that maintain consistent cognitive patterns, emotional responses, and decision-making frameworks. These agents achieve 85% human-like accuracy in behavioral simulation.

Core workflow: automatically builds consumer personas → conducts interviews with those personas → analyzes patterns to reveal emotional and cognitive factors behind human choices.

Key capabilities:
- Simulate thousands of consumer personas simultaneously
- Test product concepts, messaging, and pricing before launch
- Compress market research from weeks to minutes
- Generate both qualitative and quantitative insights
- Replace traditional surveys, focus groups, and consumer panels

Design philosophy (The Less AI, the More AI): The product simulates humans, so the brand itself must feel deeply human. The less it looks like AI, the more powerfully AI it is. Avoids: neon gradients, drop shadows, stock photo composites, 3D rendered objects.

Growth: 100K+ users. Built by XD (web3nomad, CTO of Tezign) and team.
