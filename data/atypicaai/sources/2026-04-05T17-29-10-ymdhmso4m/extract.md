# Extract

## Summary
atypica.ai is an AI research agent that simulates consumer behavior using synthetic consumers called Real Person Agents, achieving 85% human-like accuracy. The platform automatically builds consumer personas, conducts interviews, and analyzes patterns to reveal emotional and cognitive factors behind human choices, replacing traditional surveys and focus groups.

## Key Points
- Uses Real Person Agents that maintain consistent cognitive patterns, emotional responses, and decision-making frameworks with 85% human-like accuracy
- Automatically builds consumer personas and conducts interviews to analyze behavioral patterns
- Can simulate thousands of consumer personas simultaneously for testing product concepts, messaging, and pricing
- Compresses market research from weeks to minutes while generating both qualitative and quantitative insights
- Follows 'The Less AI, the More AI' design philosophy - the brand feels human rather than typically AI
