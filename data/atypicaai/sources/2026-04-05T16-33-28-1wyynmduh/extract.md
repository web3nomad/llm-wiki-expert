# Extract

## Summary
atypica.ai is an AI research platform that simulates consumer behavior using AI-powered synthetic consumers to help brands understand how different segments react to products, messaging, and pricing. The platform offers key capabilities including simulating thousands of personas simultaneously, testing product concepts before launch, and generating both qualitative and quantitative insights at a fraction of traditional research costs.

## Key Points
- AI research agent that simulates consumer behavior through synthetic consumers
- Replaces traditional market research methods like surveys, focus groups, and consumer panels
- Can simulate thousands of consumer personas simultaneously
- Enables testing of product concepts before launch
- Generates qualitative and quantitative insights for brands and market researchers
- Built by a team including XD (web3nomad), a serial entrepreneur and CTO
- Has reached over 100,000 users
