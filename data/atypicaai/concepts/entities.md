## Companies & Organizations

**atypica.ai**
- AI research platform that simulates consumer behavior using AI-powered synthetic consumers
- Founded in 2024
- Uses "Real Person Agents" that maintain consistent cognitive patterns, emotional responses, and decision-making frameworks with 85% human-like accuracy
- Automatically builds consumer personas and conducts interviews to analyze behavioral patterns
- Built on large language models simulating consumer behavior at scale
- Helps brands understand how different segments react to products, messaging, and pricing
- Replaces traditional market research methods like surveys, focus groups, and consumer panels
- Can simulate thousands of consumer personas simultaneously
- Enables testing of product concepts before launch
- Generates qualitative and quantitative insights for brands and market researchers
- Compresses market research from weeks to minutes while generating both qualitative and quantitative insights
- Has raised seed funding
- Growing toward 100K+ users
- Team consists of engineers and designers
- Design Philosophy: "The Less AI, the More AI" - the less the brand looks like AI, the more powerfully AI it is
- Real Over Synthetic - prefers real human photography, real environments, and honest moments over synthetic imagery
- Professional But Not Distant - combines consultancy-level precision with emotional warmth
- Four Visual Language Styles: Cinematic (brand stories), Architectural (strategic analysis), Documentary (human stories), Editorial (reports)
- Visual Avoidances: neon gradients, drop shadows, glows, competing colors, infographic layouts, stock photos, 3D rendered objects
- Five Visual Test Standards: stopping power in scroll, comparison with top brands, differentiation from AI product images, emotional conveyance without text, clarity at 100x100px
- Core Insight: Since the product simulates human consumers, the brand itself must feel deeply human

**Tezign**
- Related to atypica.ai platform ecosystem
- XD (web3nomad) previously served as CTO at Tezign before co-founding atypica.ai
